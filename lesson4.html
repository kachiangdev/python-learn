üî§ What is String Manipulation?

String manipulation is the process of modifying, combining, and formatting text in Python. Think of it as having a toolbox for working with words and sentences - you can change their appearance, combine them, or extract specific parts.

  • Escaping - Include special characters like quotes

Common String Methods

  • .upper() - Convert to uppercase
  • .lower() - Convert to lowercase
  • .capitalize() - Capitalize first letter
  • .title() - Capitalize each word
  • .strip() - Remove whitespace from ends
  • .replace(old, new) - Replace text
